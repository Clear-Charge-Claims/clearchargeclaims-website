Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mis-sold energy contract?

This is when a broker fails to disclose hidden commissions or misrepresents the terms of an energy deal, leading to financial loss for your business.

Who can make a claim?

Any UK business that has used an energy broker in the last 6 years could be eligible to make a claim.

How much does it cost?

We operate on a no win, no fee basis. If we don’t recover any money, you don’t pay a penny.

How long does the process take?

Most claims are processed within 6-12 weeks, but this can vary depending on the complexity of the case.

What information do I need to provide?

We’ll need basic business information and any contracts or broker communication you have available.
