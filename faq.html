Frequently Asked Questions

What do you do?

We help UK businesses reclaim hidden broker fees on their energy contracts—no win, no fee.

What documents do I need?

You’ll need an energy supply contract, a recent bill, a signed Letter of Authority, and ID of a director.

What’s your fee?

We take a 25% success fee—only if you win. If your claim is unsuccessful, you pay nothing.

Is this legal?

Yes. We operate under legal and regulatory standards and only proceed with valid, compliant claims.
