About Clear Charge Claims

Clear Charge Claims was founded to help businesses reclaim hidden commissions charged by energy brokers. We operate on a no-win, no-fee basis, which means there is no risk to you when making a claim.

Our Mission

To bring transparency and fairness to the energy market by recovering unfair charges businesses were never made aware of.
