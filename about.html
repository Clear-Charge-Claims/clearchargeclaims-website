About Clear Charge Claims

Clear Charge Claims was founded to help UK businesses recover money lost to hidden commissions in energy contracts. Many companies are unaware that energy brokers often receive substantial commissions without disclosure. We're here to change that.

Our Mission

To bring transparency to the business energy market by supporting businesses in reclaiming unfair fees and holding brokers accountable.

Our Team

Our experienced claims specialists are dedicated to navigating the complex energy sector and ensuring our clients receive the refunds they’re entitled to.

  • No win, no fee – you have nothing to lose.
  • Expert support throughout your claim.
  • Fast, efficient, and transparent service.
